More about me

I'm passionate about good web development practices and that doesn't just mean standards. I'm am an advocate for anything or anyone that contributes to good quality web development.

I've worked on large government sites as well as the smallest community sites. I develop apps, JavaScript libraries, CSS frameworks and all manner of sites small and complex.

Much of my work has been for private business but my goal now is to make more of my work publicly available.

My main strength are in working with JavaScript, HTML, CSS but I will happily work with almost any language or framework. I have my favourite tools but always use the one for the job.
